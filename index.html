Privacy Policy for iApplied

Introduction

Welcome to iApplied! This privacy policy explains how we handle data in our offline-only iOS app designed to help users track job applications and manage their CV data efficiently. Your privacy is very important to us, and we ensure that no personal data is collected or shared.

What Data We Collect

iApplied does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All job application data and CV information are stored **locally** on your device using GRDB (SQLite). No data is shared with external servers or third parties.

Permissions

iApplied only requests permission for **local notifications**. These notifications are used to remind you about follow-up actions for job applications. The notifications are managed entirely on your device and are not shared or transmitted externally. No other permissions are requested or required.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted to external servers and everything is stored locally on your device, your job application information remains secure and private. There is no risk of data breaches involving your personal information.

Third-Party Services

iApplied does not integrate with any third-party services for data collection, storage, or sharing. There are no ads, analytics, or external data transfers in the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with the "Last updated" date at the bottom.

Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please feel free to contact me, Muhammed Mahmood.
